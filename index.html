Hello and welcome to THVMWC's official website! Here you will find information on the company, information on how to pay your bill, current events happening in the water world, and more!

Our community is precious to us, and we are proud to uphold our commitment to provide clean, great tasting drinking water to all our neighbors here in the valley.



As many of you know, clean, plentiful water is something that is very easy to take for granted. Although seasonal precipitation levels change for better or worse, we always encourage you to minimize waste by fixing (and reporting!) leaks, reducing shower times, and planting drought resistant landscaping. For any questions or if you would like to reach out to us, please visit our contact page. Hope to see you all around the neighborhood!


We are passionate about our water.
