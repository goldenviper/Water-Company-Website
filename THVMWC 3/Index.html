Hello and welcome to THVMWC's website. Here you will find information on the company, how to pay your bill, current events in the water world, and more! Our small community is precious to us, and we are proud to uphold our commitment to provide clean, great tasting drinking water to all our neighbors here in the valley.



As many of you know, clean, plentiful water is something very easy to take for granted. Although seasonal precipitation levels change for better or worse, we always encourage you to minimize waste by fixing (and reporting!) leaks, reducing shower times, and planting drought resistant foliage. For any questions or comments, please visit our Contact Page.
